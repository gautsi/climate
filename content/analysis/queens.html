Fossil fuel power plants in Queens¶

Objective¶

Research the fossil fuel plants in Queens, specifically their history and expansion plans.

To answer¶

  • what are the top power plants in NYC and Queens?

  • what types of fuel do they consume, and in what quantities?

Data sources¶

US Energy Information Administration (EIA)¶

I use the following data sets from US EIA:

  • plant level generation data by month 1

    • the time period specifically looked at here is 2016-2020

  • shapefiles for plant locations 2

Analysis¶

All values are for the time period 2016-2020.

NYC power plants¶

Overall generation¶

Power plants in NYC generated a total of 97,184 gigawatt hours (GWh) of electricity in the time period, an average of 1,620 GWh per month.

Generation by borough¶

Power plants in Queens accounted for 66% of generation in the time period.

Top ten NYC power plants¶

The top ten power plants in NYC by generation accounted for 97% of total NYC generation. Of those plants, 7 are in Queens.
